About Tigers

Tiger is one of the main characters in the myths of the peoples of Asia, mainly those regions that are a natural area of ​​its habitat. He is often described as the king of beasts, the master of all living land animal, a symbol of strength and the monarchy. Tiger is usually associated with military prowess, as in India, his image - military emblem.
